About the App

The Pomodoro Focus App helps you stay productive with customizable timers for focus sessions, Pomodoro cycles, and custom durations. Designed for simplicity and effectiveness.

App Features

  • Pre-set 90-minute focus sessions
  • Pomodoro mode with 25-minute cycles
  • Customizable timers for personalized workflows
  • Minimalist, user-friendly interface
  • Dark mode support
